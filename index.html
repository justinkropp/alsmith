About Alan

Al Smith is an experienced rock climber, certified personal trainer, and seasoned emcee.

A gifted but hard-working athlete, Al's passion for climbing is equaled by his dedication to helping others achieve their potential. This dedication can be seen at climbing comps and events, where Al's emcee skills serve both the crowd and the competitor. He ensures the crowd has a memorable experience and that the competitor stays focused and motivated.

"I’ll be the voice of the event - keeping the energy high and the crowd engaged."

Services

Every event is different, and my level of involvement depends on what the event requires.

Sometimes, there's not a lot of flexibility to make significant changes, and I just work with what is given to me. Other times, I contribute to the planning, assisting the event director to make small adjustments to timing and scheduling that have a huge impact.

At climbing festivals, I adjust programming to keep things fresh. I work with the A/V team and the presenters. I prep the stage, adjust the schedule, and help shape the entire experience of the main programming block at any climbing festival. Imagine a block from 6 – 10, during which any of the following might happen: Dyno Comp, Raffles, Pinatas, Egg Games, Beer Runs, Music, Dancing, Slideshow presentations, prize giveaways, fundraises, etc.
