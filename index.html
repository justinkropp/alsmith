Services

Every event is different, and my level of involvement depends on what the event director requires.

Sometimes, there's not a lot of flexibility to make significant changes, and I just work with what is given to me. Other times, I contribute to the planning, assisting the event director to make small adjustments to timing and scheduling that have huge impacts.

At climbing festivals, I have a TON of freedom to adjust programming to keep things fresh. I work with the A/V team, I work with the presenters if there are any. I prep the stage, adjust the schedule, and pretty much shape the entire experience of the “main programming” block at any climbing festival. Imagine a block from 6-10 during which any of the following might happen: Dyno Comp, Raffles, Pinatas, Egg Games, Beer Runs, Music, Dancing, Slideshow presentations, prize giveaways, fundraises, etc.
